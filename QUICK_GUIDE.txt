## 🚀 How to Load:

1. **If extension already loaded**:
   - Click refresh button (🔄) in `chrome://extensions/`

2. **If not loaded**:
   - `chrome://extensions/`
   - Click "Load unpacked"
   - Select: `C:\Users\Sri Vishnu\Extension`
